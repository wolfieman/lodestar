## Attribution

IgniteAI was built by **HP FOWA 2024 Team 4** as a collaborative effort. The team's roles
(project management, business development, marketing, financial analysis, product
management, information systems, software engineering, and infrastructure/cybersecurity)
and individual contributions are documented in `portfolio/case-study.md`.

This repository — its scaffolding, documentation, and the Python **reference
implementation** under `src/lodestar/` — is original work authored by Wolfgang Sanyer.
The reference implementation is a **clean-room rewrite** built from the team's own
architecture documentation and the IgniteAI product specification; it does **not** copy
the original team codebase (which was licensed GPL-3.0).

The IgniteAI product configuration preserved under `product/` reflects the team's joint
work and is included here with credit to the team.

> **Public release note:** This repository is private. Any public release of
> team-derived material requires the consent of the HP FOWA 2024 Team 4 members.
